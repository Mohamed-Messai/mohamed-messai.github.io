Short bio

I joined the CPE engineering school in September 2019. From 2016 to 2019, I was a Temporary Lecturer and Research Assistant at the university Lumière Lyon 2 and ERIC laboritory. I received my Ph.D. in computer science from the university of Bejaia and the university Claude Bernard Lyon 1 in 2016. I have a "Magistère" degree from the university of Bejaia and an engeneer degree in artifical intellegence from the university of Sétif.
